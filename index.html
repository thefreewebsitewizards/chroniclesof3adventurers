About Our Family

Our Journey Through Motherhood & Adventure

Welcome to our little corner of the internet! I'm a mum to two incredible boys, navigating the beautiful chaos of parenting while embracing every moment of joy and challenge that comes our way.

Our family's journey includes parenting a child with Batten's CLN1, a rare terminal illness. Through this experience, we've learned the profound importance of celebrating small victories, finding beauty in everyday moments, and the healing power of play.

This blog is our space to share our adventures in sensory play, outdoor exploration, creative projects, and the books that inspire us along the way. We hope our stories bring you comfort, inspiration, and maybe even a few ideas for your own family adventures.
